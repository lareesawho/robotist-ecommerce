How We Operate

Four principles that guide every decision — from which manufacturers we partner with to how we support a deployment.

01

Direct Source, Full Support

We buy from the factory, not through distributors. Every dollar saved on supply chain goes into deployment quality — site assessment, compliance, training, and 12-month support.

02

Deployment Over Demo

We don't sell robots that sit in a corner. Every machine we list has a deployment path — a real use case, a real integration plan, and real support behind it.

03

Australia First, Global Standard

Every robot we import meets Australian compliance requirements. ABF customs, AS/NZS safety standards, state-specific workplace signoffs — we handle the paperwork so you don't have to.

04

Speed Without Shortcuts

8–12 weeks from order to deployment. That's fast for heavy robotics. But we don't skip compliance, testing, or training to hit a date. Speed means nothing if the robot isn't ready.
