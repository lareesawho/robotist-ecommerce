What We Do

End-to-end robotics,
from source to site.

01

Procurement

We source commercial robots directly from leading Chinese manufacturers, giving Australian businesses access to the world's most advanced machines without the complexity of navigating overseas supply chains alone.

  • Direct factory relationships across 10+ manufacturers
  • Full import compliance and customs clearance
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden margins
  • Catalogue of 13 robots across 4 categories
02

Deployment

Every robot we deliver is commissioned on-site by our Sydney-based team. We handle physical installation, environment mapping, software configuration, and integration with your existing workflows.

  • On-site installation and commissioning
  • Environment mapping and route planning
  • Software configuration and network setup
  • Staff training and operational handover
03

Support

Robotics deployment doesn't end at installation. We provide ongoing maintenance, troubleshooting, and operational guidance to ensure your machines perform reliably in production environments.

  • 12-month maintenance contract with every deployment
  • Tiered support: Essential, Priority, and Premium Command
  • Remote diagnostics and on-call troubleshooting
  • Direct line to manufacturer engineering teams
