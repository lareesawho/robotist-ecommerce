Our Story

Robotist was founded in Sydney by Robert Vicencio and Lareesa Hu — two operators who saw a gap that nobody else was filling.

Australian businesses wanted access to the world's most advanced robotics. The technology existed — China's factories were producing machines that could transform hospitality, logistics, security, and healthcare. But the path from those factories to an Australian loading dock was fragmented, opaque, and intimidating.

Robert spent 21 years building businesses across China and Australia. Lareesa built category-defining brands at the intersection of technology and human behaviour. Together, they created Robotist: a platform that doesn't just sell robots — it makes them deployable.

What We Do

We are not a marketplace. We are a procurement partner, integration specialist, and support layer — all in one.

Every robot in our catalogue has been assessed for Australian regulatory compliance, workplace safety standards, and real-world deployment readiness. We don't carry machines we wouldn't stake our reputation on.

From site assessment and import compliance to physical installation, staff training, and 12-month maintenance — Robotist manages the full lifecycle.
